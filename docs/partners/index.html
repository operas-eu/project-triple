Partners

TRIPLE consists of a consortium of 22 partners from 15 European countries and is coordinated from France by Huma-Num, a French Very Large Research Infrastructure (“Très Grande Infrastructure de Recherche”, TGIR) with international reach devoted to the Social Sciences and Humanities. Huma-Num is a unit of the French National Centre for Scientific Research (CNRS).

Your opinion is needed! Which tools would you like to have available on the GOTRIPLE platform?

We would like to hear opinions from reserachers in the social sciences and humanities fields (future GOTRIPLE users) about what kind of services we could integrate in the platform. This will help us plan for the future and make sure that the users will find the services that they need the most in GOTRIPLE.

TRIPLE is a European H2020 project that launched on 7 October 2019. At the heart of the project is the development of the GOTRIPLE platform, an innovative multilingual and multicultural discovery solution for social sciences and humanities. GOTRIPLE will provide a single access point that allows researchers in social sciences and humanities to explore, find, access and reuse materials such as literature, data, projects and researcher profiles at the European scale. Also GOTRIPLE will be integrated with specific “innovative services”, namely a Recommender System, the Trust Building System for selected users interactions, an interactive Visualisation search interface, a web Annotation Tool and a Crowdfunding platform.

Please let us know what you think by using this anonymous feedback questionnaire:

https://forms.gle/rUUkvuUGptjZcxzs9

It will just take 2 minutes of your time and it is your opportunity to help shape GOTRIPLE. The survey will remain open for 3 weeks.

Thank you for taking part!

For specific questions regarding the methodology of the survey, please contact Dr. Stefano De Paoli, Abertay University (s.depaoli[at]abertay.ac.uk).

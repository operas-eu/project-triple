GoTriple Ambassadors

Meet the GoTriple Ambassadors

GoTriple Ambassadors are social sciences and humanities researchers and engineers with open science at heart.

Together, we are building a unique network of stakeholders committed to making open research practices and SSH research outputs accessible to all.

What do GoTriple Ambassadors do ?

🤝

They connect with the community and advocate for open research practices

🔎

They help users discover the advantages of using the GoTriple platform for research and collaboration

🗣

They share their feedback on the unique features of GoTriple

Are you enthusiastic about sharing scientific knowledge, connecting with peers and building a community around shared values?
